CEABOPPL is a Proprietary Limited company incorporated 16 September 2021, for the sole purpose of raising money from the Australian public, to pay for the plant & equipment required to take climate action.

No more committee’s and reports, the technology is here now! Energy harvested from the sun, can be used to split the 2 hydrogen molecules and the oxygen molecule, in water. The stored hydrogen can be released through “fuel cells” – after the sunsets or on rainy days – to supply zero-emission electricity and zero-emission fuel for transport, every hour of everyday, of every week, of every year.

For full disclosure, the Director of CEABOPPL manufacture Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) water electrolysers and containerised balance-of-plants, which produce green hydrogen and zero-emission electricity.

According to the Climate Change Council, carbon emissions – from the generation of electricity in Australia – account for 33% of Australia’s total carbon emissions.

According to the University of Wollongong, carbon emissions – from the transport sector in Australia – account for 17.9% of Australia’s total carbon emissions.
